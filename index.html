About Manudha Thiyunuwan

Hello, I'm Manudha, a passionate photographer and videographer specializing in aerial and drone work. My journey began with a fascination for seeing the world from a new perspective, and that passion has driven me to capture breathtaking landscapes, dynamic events, and unique moments from above.

I believe that every photo tells a story. With a keen eye for detail and a commitment to quality, I strive to create visually stunning and emotionally resonant images for my clients. Whether it's a promotional video for a resort, a scenic tour of a city, or a special event, I am dedicated to delivering a one-of-a-kind visual experience.
